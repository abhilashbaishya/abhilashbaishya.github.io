Abhilash Baishya, 19 is a photographer and an aspiring filmmaker based out of Guwahati, Assam. Abhilash started taking photography seriously once he passed out of high school and since then there’s been no stopping him. His work gained recognition once he was made a suggested user by Instagram and at the same time he started getting featured by VSCO. Currently, he’s been into video a lot and has been building up his YouTube channel and covering music concerts for WhatsTheScene? in his free time.
